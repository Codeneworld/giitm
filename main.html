Welcome to GIITM

GIITM is dedicated to providing top-notch computer education, offering a range of courses such as ADCA, MDCA, DCA, Tally, and Photoshop . Our aim is to empower students with essential digital skills that meet the demands of today's technology-driven job market. We envision a learning environment that fosters creativity, innovation, and practical knowledge, ensuring our graduates are well-equipped to excel in their careers. At GIITM , we believe in bridging the gap between education and industry, preparing our students for a successful future in the world of technology.

OUR COURSES

DCA

The DCA course in computers covers essential digital skills, including software applications, data management, and basic programming, preparing students for various careers.

ADCA

ADCA (Advanced Diploma in Computer Applications) covers essential computer skills, software applications, and programming, preparing students for various IT careers.

MDCA

MDCA (Master Diploma in Computer Applications) provides advanced knowledge in programming, software development, and IT management for aspiring IT professionals.

TALLY

Tally course focuses on accounting software skills, teaching financial management, taxation, and inventory control, essential for careers in finance and accounting.

PHOTOSHOP

Photoshop course teaches photo editing, graphic design, and digital artwork techniques, equipping students with essential skills for creative industries.
