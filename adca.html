ADCA

The ADCA (Advanced Diploma in Computer Applications) course provides in-depth knowledge of computer applications and their practical uses in the professional world.

Course Content

This course covers essential topics such as MS Office, programming languages, web development, and database management, equipping students with valuable skills.

Career Opportunities

Graduates of the ADCA course can pursue various career paths in IT, including roles in software development, web design, and data management.
