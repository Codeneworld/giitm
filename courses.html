ADCA

The ADCA (Advanced Diploma in Computer Applications) course provides in-depth knowledge of computer applications and their practical uses in the professional world.

Course Content

This course covers essential topics such as MS Office, programming languages, web development, and database management, equipping students with valuable skills.

Career Opportunities

Graduates of the ADCA course can pursue various career paths in IT, including roles in software development, web design, and data management.


MDCA

The MDCA (Master Diploma in Computer Applications) course offers advanced training in computer applications, focusing on both theoretical concepts and practical skills.

Course Curriculum

The curriculum includes topics like advanced programming, web technologies, database management, and software development methodologies, ensuring a comprehensive understanding of the field.

Career Prospects

Graduates of the MDCA program are well-prepared for various roles in IT, including software engineering, system analysis, and project management.


Tally

The Tally course provides comprehensive training in Tally ERP 9, a leading software for accounting and inventory management.

Course Content

This course covers essential topics such as financial accounting, inventory management, GST compliance, and payroll management, enabling you to handle real-world accounting tasks effectively.

Career Opportunities

Completing the Tally course opens up various career paths in accounting, finance, and business management, making you a valuable asset to any organization.


Photoshop

The Photoshop course offers comprehensive training in Adobe Photoshop, a leading software for graphic design and photo editing.

Course Content

This course covers essential topics such as photo manipulation, retouching, graphic design techniques, and digital painting, equipping you with the skills to create stunning visuals.

Career Opportunities

Completing the Photoshop course can lead to various career paths in graphic design, marketing, and digital content creation, making you a sought-after professional in the creative industry.
