MDCA

The MDCA (Master Diploma in Computer Applications) course offers advanced training in computer applications, focusing on both theoretical concepts and practical skills.

Course Curriculum

The curriculum includes topics like advanced programming, web technologies, database management, and software development methodologies, ensuring a comprehensive understanding of the field.

Career Prospects

Graduates of the MDCA program are well-prepared for various roles in IT, including software engineering, system analysis, and project management.
